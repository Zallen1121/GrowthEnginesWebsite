What's Inside

Five Engines.
One Blueprint.

Every chapter is built around one of the five engines that determine whether a church or nonprofit thrives — or stalls.

01
The WHY Engine

This engine defines the WHY behind what you are doing. It gives your organization meaning and purpose — the foundation everything else is built on.

02
The WHERE Engine

This engine serves the mission by answering the critical question: "Where are we going?" Everyone wants to know the direction — this engine points the way.

03
The Resource Engine

The mission and vision can't operate without it. This is the fueling agent — it cranks everything forward, equips with tools, and protects the mission from blowing up.

04
The HOW Engine

WHY and WHERE don't tell you how to get there. This engine lays out the systems and instructions for exactly how your mission moves forward.

05
The HEALTH Engine

This engine governs what is allowed — and what is not. It sets the standard for how your team interacts, behaves, and moves forward together.
